# Downbad

**Talk yourself out of doomscrolling.**

Downbad locks the apps you say you want to use less. To open one, you have to open Downbad and say a phrase you wrote yourself — out loud, on camera. The friction of begging your phone for Instagram is the entire point.

- 🔒 Block any app on your iPhone via Apple's Screen Time framework
- 🎤 Set a custom unlock phrase per app — make it as embarrassing as you want
- 📷 Speak it on camera. Apple's on-device speech recognition verifies the match.
- ⏱️ Choose how long the unlock lasts: 5 minutes to rest of day. Re-locks automatically.
- 🔐 100% on-device. No accounts, no analytics, no servers, no data ever leaves your phone.
